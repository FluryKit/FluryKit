About Me

Get to know the artist behind the foxes! 🌿

Hey there, thank you so much for visiting!! ♡

You can call me Kit or Kovy and I go by any pronouns :D

I’m an Indonesian digital artist who loves drawing cute and expressive characters, especially cartoony animals!

I’m currently a college student and my dream is to make art my full-time job one day. Commissions and store support truly mean so much to me!! They directly help me support my academic studies and my family! I’m incredibly grateful for everyone who chooses to join and support my work.

I’m always happy and excited to interact with my followers, especially over on Instagram. Thank you for being here and supporting my art journey! ♡

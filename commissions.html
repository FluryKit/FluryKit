Terms of Service 📜

🌿 Credit & Usage 🌿

  • Credit is required when using my art where possible.
  • Do not claim my designs as your own. Always credit me as the original designer.

🌿 Turnaround Time 🌿

  • Once I start working on your commission, it usually takes 1–2 weeks or less to complete.
  • During high demand, it may take up to 1 month.
  • In case of personal or emergency situations, it may take longer, depending on complexity and my queue.
  • I will keep you updated, and you’re always welcome to ask for updates!! :D

🌿 Communication 🌿

  • Please check your notifications regularly for updates.
  • I will not continue the commission without confirmation at required stages. This may affect the final completion time.
  • If there is no response for 3 months after multiple contact attempts and the commission has already been paid or started, it will be canceled with no refund.
  • If payment has not been made yet and there is no response for 2 weeks, the request will be canceled.

🌿 Refunds & Cancellations 🌿

  • If payment has not been made yet, you may cancel your request at any time!
  • If I need to cancel due to personal reasons or emergencies, a partial refund may be given depending on the progress.
  • Refund amounts depend on the current stage (sketch, lineart, coloring, etc).
  • Clients are responsible for their own financial decisions. Refunds will not be issued for personal financial issues.
  • No refunds will be given after the sketch has been approved.

🌿 Changes & Revisions 🌿

  • Some commissions may not allow major changes, so please read the description carefully.
  • Minor fixes after completion are allowed (for example: small marking or color mistakes).

🌿 Design Rules 🌿

  • Please do not heavily modify the design. Minor edits are fine as long as the original design is still recognizable and credited.
  • Do not create characters that look very similar to my designs.
  • Breedables are not allowed, unless the offspring has major differences from the original.
  • Please do not increase the design’s worth unless additional artwork has been added.

Terms of Service

Credit & Usage

  • Credit is required when using my art whenever possible.
  • Do not claim my designs as your own. Always credit me as the original designer.

Turnaround Time

  • Commission time depends on my current queue length.
  • Once I have reached your request and started working on it, it usually takes 1–2 weeks or less to complete.
  • During high demand, it may take up to 1 month even after your request has been reached.
  • In cases of personal issues, emergencies or very complex commissions, it may take longer.
  • I will always keep you updated during the process and you’re welcome to ask for progress updates anytime!! :D

Communication

  • Please check your notifications regularly for updates.
  • I will not continue working on a commission without confirmation at required stages, which may affect completion time.
  • If there is no response for 6 months after multiple contact attempts and the commission has already been paid or started, it will be canceled with no refund.
  • If payment has not been made yet and there is no response for 2 weeks, the request will be canceled.

Refunds & Cancellations

  • If payment has not been made yet, you may cancel your request at any time!
  • If I am unable to continue due to serious personal emergencies or situations where I cannot work at all, refunds will be given depending on progress. If no work has been started, a full refund will be given.
  • Once payment is made and the commission is confirmed, the client is responsible for their own financial decision and refunds will not be issued.

Changes & Revisions

  • Some commissions may not allow major changes, so please read the commission details carefully before ordering.
  • Minor fixes after completion are allowed (such as small color mistakes or missing details).

Design Rules

  • Please do not heavily modify the design. Minor edits are fine as long as the original design is still recognizable and credited.
  • Please do not create designs that are identical or very similar to my original designs.
  • Breedables and offspring are allowed and may naturally share similarities with parent designs.
  • Please do not increase the design’s worth unless additional artwork has been added.
